Chicken margherita

Description

Grilled marinated chicken, topped with melted mozzarella, pesto, and cherry tomatoes makes a delicious summer meal! Serve with rice or angel hair pasta.

Ingredients

This recipe needs chicken breasts,olive oil,balsamic vinegar,clove garlic, minced, Italian seasoning, salt, cherry tomatoes, basil leaves, lemon juice,black pepper, mozzarella cheese and basil pesto.

  • 4 (6 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup halved cherry tomatoes
  • 4 fresh basil leaves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 4 slices mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup basil pesto
